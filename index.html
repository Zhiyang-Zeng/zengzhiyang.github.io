My name is Zhiyang (Paul) Zeng. I'm a postdoctoral researcher working with Prof. Andrea Cavalleri at the Max Planck Institute for the Structure and Dynamics of Matter. My research focuses on ultrafast light–matter interactions, aiming to uncover new pathways for controlling structural and electronic orders in complex materials.

Research Interests

  • Ultrafast dynamics
  • Light–matter interactions
  • Light-induced phase transitions
  • Emergent phenomena in condensed matter systems
