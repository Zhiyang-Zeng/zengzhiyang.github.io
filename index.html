About

My name is Zhiyang (Paul) Zeng. I am a postdoctoral researcher working with Prof. Andrea Cavalleri at the Max Planck Institute for the Structure and Dynamics of Matter. My research focuses on ultrafast light–matter interactions, aiming to uncover novel pathways for controlling structural and electronic orders in quantum materials. I use table-top ultrafast optical techniques together with scattering experiments at large-scale facilities to explore and engineer emergent functionalities.

Email: zhiyang.zeng@mpsd.mpg.de

Research Interests

  • Ultrafast dynamics
  • Light–matter interactions
  • Light-induced phase transitions
  • Emergent phenomena in condensed matter systems
