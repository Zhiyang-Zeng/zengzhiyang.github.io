About

I am an experimental condensed matter physicist working with Prof. Andrea Cavalleri. I'm interested in light–matter interactions on ultrafast timescales. My research aims to uncover novel pathways for manipulating structural and electronic orders in materials with light and to engineer emergent functionalities. I employ table-top ultrafast optical approaches as well as scattering techniques at large-scale facilities.

Email: zhiyang.zeng@mpsd.mpg.de

Research Interests

  • Light-induced chirality
  • Ferroaxial order
  • Ultrafast pump–probe spectroscopy
  • Non-equilibrium phases in solids
