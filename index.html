My name is Zhiyang (Paul) Zeng. I'm a postdoctoral researcher working with Prof. Andrea Cavalleri. My research focuses on ultrafast light–matter interactions, aiming to uncover novel pathways for controlling structural and electronic orders in quantum materials. I use table-top ultrafast optical techniques together with scattering experiments at large-scale facilities to explore and engineer emergent functionalities.

Research Interests

  • Ultrafast dynamics
  • Light–matter interactions
  • Emergent phenomena
